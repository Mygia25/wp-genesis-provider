Our Story

We Built This Because Family Matters

Genesis Provider Services was born from a simple truth: when someone you love needs care, you want a person who actually cares — not just a worker checking a box. The Okendu family has deep roots in Texas healthcare, spanning pharmacy, home health, and adult companion services. Care isn't just what we do. It's who we are.

We watched how impersonal the home care industry could be — strangers cycling in and out, caregivers following a checklist but missing the human being in the room. We knew there had to be a better way. Genesis was built on a different model: hire people we know, train them to lead with warmth, and show up fully every single day.

Today we serve families across Katy, Sugar Land, Richmond, Cinco Ranch, and West Houston — and every client we take on becomes part of our extended family.

Our Mission

Compassionate Care, Every Day — to provide personalized home care services that honor the dignity of every individual, delivered by caregivers who treat every client like their own family.

Pharmacy Background

PharmD Expertise

Our team includes members with Doctor of Pharmacy credentials — meaning we understand medications, interactions, and health monitoring at a clinical level that most home care providers simply don't have.

Home Health Background

Home Health Aide

Certified Home Health Aide experience means our providers know how to deliver personal care, assist with ADLs (activities of daily living), and respond to health changes — with skill and calm.

Companion Care Background

Adult Companion Services

We know that companionship is care too. Our experience in adult companion services means we understand the emotional and social needs of our clients — not just the physical ones.
