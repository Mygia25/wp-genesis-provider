### Requirements for PAS License in Texas (Reference)
Per HHSC, a PAS agency must:
1. Complete pre-survey computer-based training
2. Complete license application with all documents
3. Pay required fees
4. Be registered with TX Comptroller (good standing)
5. Be registered with TX Secretary of State (good standing)
6. Be approved by HHS/HCSSA Licensure & Certification Unit
7. Have an Administrator + Alternate Administrator
8. Administrator qualifications: physician, RN, social worker, or licensed NFA admin, OR high school diploma/GED + 2 years supervisory/management in health setting OR 1 year caregiver experience OR 2 years college in health field
9. Processing time: up to 45 days after complete application

## 5. SERVICES ANALYSIS

### What "Personal Care Attendant" Means (Taxonomy 3747P1801X)

A Personal Care Attendant provides **non-medical, in-home assistance** with:
- Eating / meal preparation
- Bathing / personal hygiene
- Dressing
- Toileting
- Activities of Daily Living (ADLs)
- Light housekeeping
- Laundry
- Bed mobility assistance
- Companionship
- Basic transportation / errands

### Key Distinction: Personal Care vs. Home Health
| Feature | Personal Care Attendant (PAS) | Home Health Care (HHSS) |
|---------|-------------------------------|-------------------------|
| **Medical care** | NO — non-medical only | YES — skilled nursing, therapy |
| **Staff licensing** | No clinical license required | RN, LPN, PT, OT required |
| **Oversight** | Administrator (see qualifications above) | Physician oversight required |
| **Medicaid programs** | PHC (Primary Home Care), CAS (Community Attendant Services) | Licensed Home Health Services |
| **HCSSA category** | Personal Assistance Services | Licensed Home Health / Licensed & Certified |
| **Typical clients** | Elderly, disabled, need ADL help | Post-hospital, wound care, therapy |

### Texas Medicaid Programs Genesis Could Serve

1. **Primary Home Care (PHC):** For TX residents 21+ receiving SSI. Up to 50 hours/week of attendant services.
2. **Community Attendant Services (CAS):** For persons of all ages who don't yet qualify for Medicaid. Similar services to PHC.
3. **Consumer Directed Services (CDS):** Participant-directed option where clients choose their own attendants.
4. **Private Pay:** Direct payment from families for personal care services.

### Services to Highlight on Website
Based on the taxonomy and industry standards, the website should feature:

- Personal Care Assistance (bathing, grooming, dressing)

**Program Types:**
