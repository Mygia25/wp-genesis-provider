Our Story

We Built This Because Family Matters

Genesis Provider Services was started by the Okendu family because they understood what it means to need help — and to be afraid to ask for it. When people they loved needed care, the family saw firsthand how impersonal the system can feel. Strangers cycling in and out. Caregivers following a checklist but missing the person. Families left feeling like they chose wrong.

They knew there had to be a better way. Genesis was built on a different model — hiring people they know, friends, family members, community connections — and training them to bring that same warmth into the homes of every client they serve.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, personalized home care services that honor the dignity of every individual — delivered by caregivers who treat every client like their own family.
